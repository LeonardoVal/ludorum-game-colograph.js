Colograph

How to play

Players Red and Blue take turns painting the blank shapes. Red goes first. Two shapes may be linked by a line. A line gets a colour when the shapes it connects are both painted with this same colour.

Each coloured line that connects two identical shapes adds one point to the player's score. Each coloured line that connects two different shapes subtracts one point from the player's score. The game ends when all shapes have been coloured, and the player with the highest score wins.
